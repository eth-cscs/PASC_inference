projection onto simplex

computes a projection of a point onto simplex in nD

take K-dimensional vector x[tK,tK+1,...tK+K-1] =: p and compute projection P(p) = arg min || p - y ||_2 subject to constraints (which define simplex) y_0 + ... y_{K-1} = 1 y_i >= 0 for all i=0,...,K-1

in practical applications K is much more lower number than T K - number of clusters (2 - 10^2) T - length of time-series (10^5 - 10^9)
